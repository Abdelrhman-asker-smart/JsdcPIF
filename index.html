    Vision

    Jabal Al Shifa Development Company is set to establish an inclusive year-round touristic haven and unique vacation community in the Taif mountains, harmonizing nature, wellness, and adventure for locals, internationals, and pilgrim visitors.

    Mission

    Mission Jabal Al Shifa Development Company will develop an integrated, distinctive destination; it will champion sustainable tourism by engaging the local community, regional and international partners. It will catalyze socio-economic growth, while preserving the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the Taif region.

    Strategic Objectives

    Plan and develop the destination on time

    Activate and promote a year-round destination

    Generate attractive financial returns

    Implement sustainable tourism practices
